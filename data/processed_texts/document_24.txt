• State -owned financial  institutions  – The 
Development  Bank  of Nigeria (DBN), Bank  of Industry  
(BOI) and Nigeria  Sovereign  Investment  Authority  
(NSIA)  have  various  funds  for developmental  
investments  in Nigeria. NSIA  recently  launched  
CarbonVista for investing in carbon  market  projects .
• National  Climate  Change Fund – The Climate  
Change  Act 2021 provides  for the creation  of the 
Climate  Change  Fund  (CCF) . This is currently  under
