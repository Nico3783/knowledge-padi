Clear regulatory guidelines need to be in place
Relevant regulatory provisions related to the pilot project were reviewed at its commencement. The team met with the Nigeria Electricity Regulatory Commission (NERC) to clarify whether any regulatory gaps required addressing. This is critical, as evidence shows that many mini- grid projects in 
Africa fail because of inadequate regulatory guidance and oversight. The project team found that NERC updated its Guidelines on grid interconnection, which established specifications and procedures for interconnected mini and micro -grids.
