UKNIAF’s intervention
UKNIAF’s regional and international experts team 
supported the Rural Electrification Agency (REA) in clarifying and testing the configuration for integrating microgrids on the national grid using an existing microgrid (developed by EmONE). The project was 
executed using a multi -stage approach encompassing 
design and modelling studies, grid interconnection, and an impact study on grid stability.
Before UKNIAFs’ involvement, the mini -grid system 
actively supplied reliable, renewable power to the 
complex where the Federal Ministry of Power (formerly Federal Ministry of Power, Works, and Housing) is based. However, despite having grid -interactive
